Winter is coming
Silence is imminent
Memories are made,
Others may fade

In winter, silence is inevitable
As Fall abates to the quiet season,
Th drums of life take a break,
The souls of the resting leave the wake

Silence is the language of the grieving;
The dead may listen, but never respond
Chills touch may reap,
Another soul as they weep
